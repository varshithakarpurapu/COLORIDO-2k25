BOYS EVENTS


BasketBall
A team sport where two teams of five players each try to score points by shooting a ball through the opponent's hoop. The game involves dribbling, passing, and shooting, and is played on a rectangular court with a hoop at each end and played on a any shape ground also.


VolleyBall
A team sport where two teams of six players each try to score points by hitting a ball over a net and into the opponent's court. The game involves serving, passing, setting, attacking, blocking, and digging, and is played on a rectangular court divided by a net.


Table Tennis
A fast-paced racket sport where two or four players hit a lightweight ball back and forth across a table using small paddles. The game is played on a rectangular table with a net dividing it, and points are scored by making the ball land on the opponent's side of the table without being returned.



GIRLS EVENTS


Throw Ball
A team sport where two teams of seven players each try to score points by throwing a ball over a net and into the opponent's court. The game is similar to volleyball but allows catching and throwing the ball. It is played on a rectangular court divided by a net.


Tennikoit
A racket sport similar to tennis, but played with a rubber ring (koit) instead of a ball. Two players or teams hit the koit back and forth over a net, aiming to land it in the opponent's court. The game is played on a rectangular court divided by a net.


Table Tennis
A fast-paced racket sport where two or four players hit a lightweight ball back and forth across a table using small paddles. The game is played on a rectangular table with a net dividing it, and points are scored by making the ball land on the opponent's side of the table without being returned.
